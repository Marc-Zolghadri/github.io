Marc Zolghadri — Scientific Profile

```

Professor at ISAE-Supméca · Laboratoire Quartz · Obsolescence Management · System Engineering · Industrial Resilience

I develop scientific models, methods and tools for proactive obsolescence management, system redesign and lifecycle resilience of complex industrial systems.

Research focus

  • Proactive obsolescence management and long-lifespan systems
  • Propagation of risks, changes and decisions in complex system architectures
  • System engineering, redesign and resilience to obsolescence
  • Obsolescence forecasting, probabilistic modelling and explainable AI
  • Maintenance in operational condition, availability and critical systems

Selected research activities

  • Coordinator of ANR EOS — Active Obsolescence Management Decision Support
  • Coordinator of ANR LabCom i-OREO — Obsolescence and Optimized Resilience
  • Scientific supervision of doctoral and postdoctoral research on obsolescence
  • Contributions to IEC 62402 and international obsolescence management networks

Collaboration opportunities

  • Joint research projects: ANR, Horizon Europe, LabCom, international programs
  • PhD supervision, CIFRE theses and doctoral committees
  • Scientific publications, special issues and conference tracks
  • Academic expertise, evaluation, keynote lectures and workshops
